About

Two crafts. One discipline.

I move between product design and fiction writing — and have spent ten years learning that they're the same job. Both are about structuring journeys for people who are paying attention with feeling, not just function.

The UX Designer

Systems, accessibility, and AI

I build scalable design systems in Figma and Figma Make — leveraging variables, complex auto-layout, and robust components to deliver production-ready frameworks that bridge creative vision and code. What sets me apart is fluency in AI-native design: hands-on expertise in Claude, Claude-powered workflows, and prompt engineering to streamline complex processes and make emerging technology accessible.

The Author & Strategist

Narrative as a UX discipline

My background as the author of two bestselling novels gives me a unique perspective on experience design. Storytelling is foundational — it lets me articulate user journeys, frame design problems clearly for stakeholders, and craft compelling narratives around a product vision. The structural rigor I bring to plotting a thriller is the same I bring to mapping an edge case.
