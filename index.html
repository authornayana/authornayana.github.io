About Me

The UX Designer

I thrive on turning complex user pain points into elegant, scalable solutions. At the core of my workflow is a mastery of Figma, where I build robust design systems and high-fidelity prototypes. My methodology is rigorous: I begin with in-depth UX research and data analysis, then transition into Figma to craft precise wireframes and interactive designs. By augmenting my core design expertise with AI tools like Gemini and Figma Make, I am able to accelerate the ideation process and deliver accessible, conversion-driving experiences with greater efficiency.

The Author & Strategist

My background as an author of two bestselling novels in contemporary romance and suspense thrillers provides me with a unique perspective on user experience. I view storytelling as a foundational skill that allows me to articulate user journeys, frame design problems clearly for stakeholders, and create compelling narratives around a product vision. While my core strength is rooted in traditional Figma design, I am constantly evolving my craft by mastering emerging technologies like Figma Make and AI-driven prompt engineering to build more immersive and effective digital experiences.
