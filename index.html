About Me

The UX Designer

I specialize in transforming complex user friction into elegant, scalable digital products through a rigorous, system-first approach. At the core of my workflow is a mastery of Figma, where I don’t just design static screens—I architect robust, scalable design systems. By leveraging advanced variables, modular components, and responsive auto-layout, I ensure that every design is built for consistency and long-term maintainability. My methodology bridges the gap between deep UX research and technical execution. I utilize generative AI tools to sharpen the ideation phase, allowing me to move rapidly from data-backed insights to high-fidelity, accessible prototypes. By centering my process around a structured design system in Figma, I am able to deliver conversion-driving experiences that remain cohesive across every touchpoint and streamline the handoff from vision to production.

The Author & Strategist

My background as an author of two bestselling novels in contemporary romance and suspense thrillers provides me with a unique perspective on user experience. I view storytelling as a foundational skill that allows me to articulate user journeys, frame design problems clearly for stakeholders, and create compelling narratives around a product vision. While my core strength is rooted in traditional Figma design, I am constantly evolving my craft by mastering emerging technologies like Figma Make and AI-driven prompt engineering to build more immersive and effective digital experiences.
